Pro tips

After select the above color pattern, you can open your browser console then type in refresh() to shuffle the icons order.

It also take a integer number as parameter, for example refresh(50) will display 50 icons.


You can use a Chrome extension like awesome screenshot to capture the generated page a picture.


Octwall also contains Google's Material Design icons, which is not avaliable in this online demo but present in the repository. Clone the repo and play with it:) . You can also use your own svg icons set, checkout the repo README.md.
